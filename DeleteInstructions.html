Delete Your Account

We respect your privacy and make it easy to delete your account and all associated data. Follow these simple steps to permanently remove your information from LiftSmart.

Important Notice

Deleting your account is permanent and cannot be undone. All your data, including workout history, progress, and personal information will be permanently removed.

1

Go to Profile Tab

Open the LiftSmart app and tap the Profile tab at the bottom of the screen.

2

Scroll to the Bottom

Scroll down to the bottom of your profile page.

3

Tap Delete Account

Tap the Delete Account button and confirm your choice. Your account and all associated data will be permanently deleted.

Need Help?

If you encounter any issues during the deletion process or have questions, please contact our support team at bfitappfeedback@gmail.com
